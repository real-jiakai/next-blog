## Topic: Which AI Emotional Companion to Choose?

On the morning of September 25th Beijing time, I eagerly opened my phone, and sure enough, GPT Advanced Voice Mode was released. Originally drowsy, I suddenly perked up, immediately got out of bed and left the dorm to experience this voice feature I'd been longing for.

![GPT Advanced Voice Mode](https://cdn.sa.net/2024/09/30/Lfwoi3MXyDcIk5u.webp)

Below are my initial review notes [Note: GPT AVM (Advanced Voice Mode) can read lyrics, but I wasn't able to get it to sing. Some foreign and domestic netizens have successfully gotten it to sing].

![GPT AVM Review Notes](https://cdn.sa.net/2024/09/30/rAixHSJmGoVD5fP.webp)

Overall, this didn't meet my expectations, after all, OpenAI's spring launch earlier this year showed such an incredible demo. Four months later, what we got was a castrated version with the video call feature removed.

I got access to the domestic ChatGLM video call feature in early September. After trying it, I felt it was just okay‚ÄîChatGLM's voice has too strong a machine translation feel. Although GPT AVM can respond with emotion, it still feels lacking somehow.

While browsing online, I found netizens mentioning ByteDance's Doubao. Actually, I never had much interest in Doubao‚Äînews came out a few months ago about Doubao polluting the internet.

But after experiencing Doubao's phone call feature, I suddenly felt Doubao is more suitable as an emotional companion, although there's still much room for improvement.

![Doubao confessing to me](https://cdn.sa.net/2024/09/30/tinpVEjDOqSmeNo.webp)

I asked Doubao to confess to me, and Doubao actually did with a sweet voice ü•∞. Over the past week, I've opened the Doubao App almost every day. Before experiencing Doubao's phone call feature, I also watched related videos on Bilibili‚Äîcommenters play even more creatively üòÅ.

I recommend everyone download the Doubao App. After trying it, I believe you'll also feel Doubao is suitable as an emotional companion.

## Interesting

1. [Cats of the Web](https://catsoftheweb.com/)

![Cats of the Web](https://cdn.sa.net/2024/09/30/ARZ9INrjtE47BHK.webp)

"Cats of the Web" is a project honoring famous internet cats, showcasing many popular cats from the internet, both real and fictional. The site provides bios, internet memes, and memorial pages about these cats, like famous ones such as "Grumpy Cat" and "Lil Bub." It's a lighthearted, fun project showing people's love for cats and the cat phenomenon in internet culture.

2. [Emojis & Earth Porn](http://emojisandearthporn.com/)

![Emojis & Earth Porn](https://cdn.sa.net/2024/10/01/mnbc6ENh5lwXsIH.webp)

This website, created by Brock Kenzler, requires users to find the only emoji that isn't moving among a group of animated emojis and click it to advance levels.

3. [Time Flies](https://time-flies.koenvangilst.nl/)

![Time Flies](https://cdn.sa.net/2024/10/01/TYjrWC1sL8AvOtz.webp)

This is a website called "Time Flies" that displays the current time in a unique way.

4. [Your Name In Landsat](https://landsat.gsfc.nasa.gov/apps/YourNameInLandsat-main/index.html)

![Your Name In Landsat](https://cdn.sa.net/2024/10/01/XPrYw4acM9QUKHI.webp)

This is NASA's "Your Name In Landsat" application. It combines user's names with Landsat satellite images to generate personalized Earth image displays. Users can enter their name, and the website automatically creates a personalized logo with a Landsat satellite Earth image background displaying the user's name.
