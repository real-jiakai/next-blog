Plus, the entire class is male, so the possibility of finding a girlfriend is nearly zero. Unless you find girls from other departments with more women, but few guys have the courage to be so bold and shameless.

Seeing this, you might think my point is: Xiaohongshu really can help you find love. But you'd be wrong. On social platforms like Xiaohongshu and Douyin, the possibility of finding love is extremely slim, because there's a gap between online and reality.
